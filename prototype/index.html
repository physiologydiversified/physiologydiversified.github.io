Phases of Gastric Motility

The cephalic phase is initiated by the mere sight, smell, taste, or thought of food, even before food enters the stomach. This anticipatory response prepares the gastrointestinal tract for digestion. It accounts for approximately 20% of total gastric acid secretion.

Myasthenia Gravis

Myasthenia gravis is a chronic neuromuscular disease that causes weakness in the voluntary muscles. Voluntary muscles include muscles that connect to a person's bones, muscles in the face, throat, and diaphragm. Myasthenia gravis is an autoimmune disease
