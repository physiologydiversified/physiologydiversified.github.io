Phases of Gastric Motility

The phases of gastric motility—cephalic, gastric, and intestinal—are triggered by the sight, smell, or taste of food, starting with salivation and preparing the stomach for digestion.

Myasthenia Gravis

Depicting muscle weakness and facial drooping, this image shows how myasthenia gravis disrupts neurotransmission at the neuromuscular junction, impairing muscle contraction due to blocked acetylcholine receptors.

Negative Intrapleural Pressure

This image highlights how negative intrapleural pressure helps lungs expand by contrasting healthy, elderly, and obese lungs, illustrating the impact of body composition on respiratory function.





Respiratory Physiology 2D Diagram

Vestibular System Function

This image demonstrates how the vestibular system detects head movements using otoliths and otoconia, showing the role of the inner ear in maintaining balance and spatial orientation.

Motor Signalling Pathway and Spinal Injury Impairment

This upcoming image will illustrate the corticospinal motor pathway, showing how voluntary movement signals travel from the brain to muscles. It will also depict how spinal injuries can block these pathways, leading to motor function impairments..





Central Nervous System Interactive

Jaundice Across Skin Tones

Soon, an image will demonstrate how jaundice presents in different skin tones due to liver dysfunction and a buildup of bilirubin. This illustration will explain the physiological mechanism behind jaundice and how it affects the appearance of the skin.

Touch Sensation and Pacinian Corpuscles

An upcoming diagram will show how the sensation of touch is relayed to the brain through the nervous system, with a focus on the role of Pacinian corpuscles in the skin. It will detail how these specialized receptors detect mechanical pressure and vibration.

This image will depict the physiological changes that occur during menopause, including hormonal shifts, effects on the reproductive system, and the broader implications for health, such as bone density and cardiovascular function.

About This Project

This repository showcases inclusive and diverse educational visuals in physiology, aiming to support modern and equitable teaching strategies. All visuals are created by biomedical communicators with academic rigor and creative clarity.
